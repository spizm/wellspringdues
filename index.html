Wellspring Membership Dues

Here you will be able to pay your $60 membership dues for the virtual summer session of 2020!

The buttons below will allow you to add an additional donation of $10, $20, $25, $50, $60 and $100. This is optional and clicking NONE will pay just the $60 fee. If you choose to donate extra, it should be reflected in your contract as to where you would like this to go, either to help with scholarships for others wanting to participate, or to our general fund. This can be found in the final section of the contract form. If, for any reason, you would like to donate a different amount, select NONE and after submitting your normal payment, head to wellspringensemble.org to donate as normal.

Dues help to cover the operating costs of the ensemble which include:

  • Payment of our employees, Evan and Genevieve
  • Materials/Music/Etc.
  • Time for Audio and Video Engineering
  • Software costs
  • Social Media engagement (Boosting, etc)
  • and more!

Thank you so much for participating in the exciting virtual summer session! We will be getting you materials soon!
